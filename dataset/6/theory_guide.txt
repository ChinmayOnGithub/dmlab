DATASET 6 - BANKING CUSTOMER ANALYSIS
=====================================

ALGORITHM IMPLEMENTED:
- Naive Bayes Classification

DATASET DESCRIPTION:
- Banking customer data for deposit prediction
- Attributes: Customer type, Account number, Bank name, Location, Deposit decision
- Real-world business application dataset
- Binary classification problem (deposit: yes/no)

BUSINESS CONTEXT
===============

PROBLEM STATEMENT:
Predict whether a customer will make a deposit based on their demographic and banking information.

BUSINESS VALUE:
- Targeted marketing campaigns
- Resource allocation optimization
- Customer relationship management
- Risk assessment and profiling
- Revenue prediction and planning

WHY NAIVE BAYES FOR BANKING DATA?
=================================

DOMAIN-SPECIFIC ADVANTAGES:
1. Fast decision making for real-time applications
2. Handles categorical banking data well
3. Provides probability estimates for risk assessment
4. Works with limited customer data
5. Interpretable results for business stakeholders
6. Robust to irrelevant features (like account numbers)

BANKING INDUSTRY APPLICATIONS:
- Credit scoring and loan approval
- Fraud detection systems
- Customer churn prediction
- Product recommendation
- Risk assessment
- Regulatory compliance

FEATURE ANALYSIS FOR BANKING:
============================

RELEVANT FEATURES:
1. Customer Type (Gender): Demographic segmentation
2. Location: Geographic market analysis
3. Bank Name: Institution loyalty patterns

IRRELEVANT FEATURES:
1. Account Number: Unique identifier, no predictive value
2. Customer Name: Personal identifier, privacy concerns

FEATURE ENGINEERING CONSIDERATIONS:
